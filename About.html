[ Background ]

The path to product-focused design.

My journey began during my undergraduate studies in Digital Media Arts, a time filled with visual experimentation, including video design, game animation, and art education. While these roles were deeply creative, they sparked a realization: I was more fulfilled when a design directly solved a real-world problem than when it served purely aesthetic purposes.

This realization became the catalyst for my shift toward UX. I found that I truly thrive where creative innovation meets pragmatic, logical execution. Pursuing a Master's in UX allowed me to formalize this transition, bridging my background in media arts with the rigorous demands of product design.

Today, I approach every project with this cross-disciplinary lens, ensuring that the interfaces I build are not just usable, but are meaningful solutions for the people who rely on them.
